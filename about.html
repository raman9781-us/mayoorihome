About Us

Our Story

R&M Web Solutions began with a simple mission: to help local businesses establish a strong online presence. With Mayuri’s eye for design and Raman’s background in software delivery management, we combine creativity with technical know‑how to craft websites that work just as hard as you do.

We understand the unique challenges faced by small businesses and startups. That’s why we focus on delivering quality, affordable solutions that grow with your business. From our home base in Cincinnati, Ohio, we collaborate closely with you to ensure your website reflects your brand and connects with your audience.
